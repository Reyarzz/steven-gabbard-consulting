RemindFlow MVP

Internal Demo: Automated Customer Follow-up & Reminder Engine

Concept

RemindFlow is an internal engine designed to bridge the gap between "we sent the invoice/proposal" and "we got paid/signed." It automates the polite persistence that usually takes hours of manual checking every week.

// RemindFlow Logic Preview
IF invoice_age > 7 DAYS AND status == "unpaid":
  SEND email_template("friendly_reminder")
  LOG "7-day reminder sent to client"
ELIF invoice_age > 14 DAYS AND status == "unpaid":
  SEND email_template("urgent_followup")
  NOTIFY owner("Action required: Invoice #123")

Core Features

Tiered Escalation

Reminders get progressively more firm based on custom triggers and timelines.

Smart Pause

Automatically stops sequences the moment a payment is detected or a manual override is toggled.

Multi-Channel

Support for Email, SMS (via Twilio), and internal Slack/Teams notifications.

Audit Trail

Every touchpoint is logged back to your CRM or source spreadsheet for total visibility.
