Why KCDPA Matters to Your Dealership

The Kentucky Car Dealer Protection Act (SB 129) requires compliance for any dealer handling consumer data — and the penalties for non-compliance are severe.

  • Fines up to $7,500 per violation
  • Consumer right-to-delete requests within 45 days
  • Mandatory Data Protection Assessments
  • Audit trail documentation required
